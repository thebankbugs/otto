Introduction to Forex Trading

Free Educational Resource • The Bank Bugs Ecosystem

1. What is Forex?

Forex (Foreign Exchange) is the global marketplace for exchanging national currencies against one another. It is the largest, most liquid financial market in the world.

2. Who Operates It?

The market is run by a global network of banks, financial institutions, central banks (like the Bank of PNG), hedge funds, commercial corporations, and retail traders.

3. Flexible Time in PNG

Because the market operates 24 hours a day, 5 days a week across different global sessions (Sydney, Tokyo, London, New York), traders in PNG have total flexibility to trade in the mornings, evenings, or late at night.
